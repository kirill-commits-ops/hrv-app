💨 Resonance Breathing

Breathe at ~5.5 breaths/min (5.5s in, 5.5s out). This hits your baroreflex resonance frequency and maximally stimulates your vagus nerve. Do 10-20 min daily. This alone can add 10-15 ms to your HRV in weeks.

🧊 Cold Exposure

End your shower with 1-2 min of cold water. Cold activates the vagus nerve and trains your autonomic nervous system. Start at 30s if it's hard. Aim for face/neck/chest exposure.

😴 Sleep Optimization

HRV is mostly built during sleep. No food 3h before bed. No screens 1h before. Room at 18-19C. Consistent wake time matters more than bedtime. Aim for 7-8h.

🏋 Zone 2 Cardio

150 min/week of low-intensity cardio (walking, easy cycling, swimming where you can hold a conversation). This builds your aerobic base and parasympathetic tone. Don't overdo high-intensity — it temporarily tanks HRV.

🍷 Cut Alcohol

Even 1-2 drinks crush HRV for 24-48 hours. If you drink, notice the pattern on your tracker. This is often the single biggest lever people miss.

☕ Caffeine Timing

No caffeine after 1 PM. Morning coffee is fine but late caffeine disrupts deep sleep stages where HRV recovery happens. Half-life is 5-6 hours.

🧘 Vagus Nerve Hacks

Gargling vigorously, humming/chanting, and slow exhales all stimulate the vagus nerve directly. Try humming for 5 min or gargling water hard enough to make your eyes tear up.
